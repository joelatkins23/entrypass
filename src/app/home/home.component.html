Entrypass: A digital public health and safety solution for all.

Entrypass brings individuals inner peace when going out shopping for essentials. Sharing your visitor details helps us to protect you and the more vulnerable persons in our society.

Entrypass allows visitors to check-in to business places smoothly. It greatly reduces the work needed to collect and safely store visitor entry-exit data. Entrypass allows businesses to focus on value-added activities while adhering to national protocols.

Entrypass also supports approved health institutions (e.g. Ministry of Health) by providing a full-scale, user-friendly, digital tool to optimize contact tracing efforts.

Entrypass helps in the fight against infectious viruses.

How it works

Get unique visitor ID

Individuals create their user profile and receive a unique visitor ID which protects their personal data. Visitor ID is then used to privately and securely check-in and out at different venues.

Scan, Enter and Enjoy

Businesses collect visitor data via a safe and automated system which protects visitor privacy

Protect public health, safety and wellness

Visitor data collected at each venue point is automatically transferred to the Health Authorities for public health & safety purposes only (e.g. contact tracing).
